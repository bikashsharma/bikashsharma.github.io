Welcome !

I am Bikash Sharma, currently infrastructure engineer at Facebook. Before that, I was a PhD candidate in the department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Pennsylvania State University.
, where I was a member of High Performance Computing Lab, advised by professor Chita R. Das.
My research interests include design of high performance and energy-efficient distributed systems like Internet Data Centers and Clouds.

On a personal note, I was born and brought up in a small town called Cuttack in India. Prior to joining Penn State in Fall 2007, I completed my undergraduate studies in Computer Science and Engineering from National Institute of Technology Rourkela in 2007.

Research

I am interested in the broad area of distributed systems and networks. Specifically, my research interests and focus include:

  • Characterization and modeling of workloads for distributed systems like clouds and data centers.
  • Improving the performance and power efficiency of data centers and clouds.
  • Big Data Analytics; MapReduce
  • Virtualization
  • Scheduling and resource management in large-scale MapReduce clusters
  • Problem determination and fault diagnosis in virtualized clouds

More details can be found in my list of Publications below.
